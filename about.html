KUHUSU TAUSI/ABOUT TAUSI

TAUSI IS A SWAHILI PROGRAMMING LANGAUAGE THAT WAS CREATED FOR THE SOLE PURPOSE FOR TANZANIANS TO BE ABLE TO HAVE THEIR OWN WAY OF MAKING COMPUTER PROGRAMS, PROVIDED WITH AN EASY AND SIMPLE SYNTAX, THE IDEA OFFICIALLY ORIGINATED FROM CHRIS MASAKO, THE PIONEER INNOVATOR OF THE TAUSI PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE,HE THEN GATHERED A TEAM OF HIS FRIENDS AND THEY ALL DECIDED TO PUT IN THEIR EFFORT AND COMMITMENT TO HELP ALL TANZANIANS ACHIEVE THEIR FIRST PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE
